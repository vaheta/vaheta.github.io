About Me

Hey there! I'm a Senior Imaging Scientist at Intrinsic, where I help robots see. Fingers crossed that once they see the beauty of the world, they'll reconsider taking over!

I focus on research in 3D computer vision, work on fun side projects, and occasionally write down my ideas. Feel free to explore!
